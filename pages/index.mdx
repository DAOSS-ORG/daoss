# Shu Ding

Shu (b. 1994 China, based in Berlin) is a designer and developer focusing on visual ideas.

Started programming in high school and has since spent 4 years on **algorithm competitions**, Shu worked as a **graphic designer and web developer** in Shanghai, and got his BSc degree in **Computer Science** from Fudan University. Shu has been contributing to the **open source** community since 2013. He also enjoys creating **games, creative coding and HCI projects**.
